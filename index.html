SERVICES

After School Program

Its hard getting help with homework especially when you dont understand it all. It is our team's mission to make sure that our scholars look forward to going to school. In afterschool our students can take so much more than academic help but also social and communication skills that they can take with them into the world.

Saturday Classes

Saturday classes will be held to provided homework assistance and study tactics. Our scholars will be to recieve help in all areas from math and science to english and history. Saturday classes can help our students get a better understanding of the curriculum they are learning at their highschools to better prepare them for college.

SAT Prep

SAT classes will be offered to help our scholars be better prepared for the Math, Reading, Writing & Language, and Essay components of the SAT exam. Our team has had great success with helping scholars achieve outstanding scores on their SAT exams, and we believe we can do the same for you. Invest in your future.

College Counseling

It is in our best interest to make sure our scholars know everything about applying to colleges and what they should and should not do in the process. Advice on anything from college essays to financial planning. We will ensure our scholars have all the means to graduate highschool and follow their dreams at the college of their choice.

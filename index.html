It Takes A Village

It Takes A Village is a non profit that aims to close the education gap that exist across the New England Area. We believe that by hosting rigorous classes in math and english throughout the school year our students will be able to better prepare for the SAT, and College application process. Additionally we believe that it takes a village to ensure that our students are equipped with the tools and skills necessary to succeed in the world.

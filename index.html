It Takes A Village is a non profit that aims to close the education gap that exist across the New England Area. We believe that by hosting rigorous classes in Math and English throughout the school year our students will be able to better prepare for the SAT, and College application process. Additionally we believe that it takes a village to ensure that our students are equipped with the tools and skills necessary to succeed in the world.

SERVICES

After School Program

In afterschool, our students can take academic help, as well as social and communication skills with them into the world.

Saturday Classes

These are held to provide homework assistance in all subjects, study tactics, and to help our students grasp what they learn at their high schools.

SAT Prep

SAT classes will be offered to help our scholars be better prepared for the Math, Reading, Writing & Language, and Essay components of the SAT exam. Our team has had great success with helping scholars achieve outstanding scores on their SAT exams, and we believe we can do the same for you. Invest in your future.

College Counseling

It is in our best interest to make sure our scholars know everything about applying to colleges and what they should and should not do in the process. Advice on anything from college essays to financial planning. We will ensure our scholars have all the means to graduate highschool and follow their dreams at the college of their choice.

It Takes A Village is a non profit that aims to close the education gap that exist across the New England Area. We believe that by hosting rigorous classes in Math and English throughout the school year our students will be able to better prepare for the SAT, and College application process. Additionally we believe that it takes a village to ensure that our students are equipped with the tools and skills necessary to succeed in the world.

SERVICES

After School Program

In afterschool, our students can utilize academic help, as well as social and communication skills with them into the world. This reinforces what our students are learning.

Saturday Classes

These are held to provide homework assistance in all subjects, study tactics, and to help our students grasp what they learn at their high schools.

SAT Prep

Our SAT classes offer preparation on its Math, Reading, Writing & Language, and Essay components, and has been successful in helping students score high.

College Counseling

We guide our scholars through the process of applying to colleges. From essays to finances, we ensure our scholars follow their dreams with their college choices.
